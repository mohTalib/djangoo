About Us

  • Who We Are

    We are a group of active people who aim to build a community in which we share experiences, build connections and partake in local and international events such as conferences, internships, campaigns and so much more.

  • Our Mission

    Our goal is to encourage youth to be active by building a community of like-minded individuals who share and collaborate to improve essential skills required in the workforce and life. This community aims to implement changes in line with the Sustainable Development Goals to ensure Iraq keeps up with the developed nations.

Our Activities

  • Skill-Building Workshops:

    “Organize skill-building workshops and training sessions for the youth community. These workshops could cover a wide range of topics, such as communication skills, leadership development, project management, and digital literacy. By providing practical training, the group can help young people acquire valuable skills for both the workforce and life.”

  • Local and International Conferences

    “Host or participate in local and international conferences related to youth development, sustainable goals, and social impact. Attending such conferences can provide members with exposure to global perspectives, networking opportunities, and the chance to learn from experts in various fields.”

  • Community Campaigns

    “Initiate community campaigns focused on addressing local issues aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These campaigns could involve environmental conservation, education access, poverty alleviation, or health promotion. Engaging in hands-on projects that contribute to the betterment of their local community will not only create a positive impact but also foster a sense of purpose among the youth.”
